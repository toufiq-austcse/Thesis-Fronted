About

Greenness analysis refers to the analysis of forestation and green areas of a certain area. Remote sensing using satellite imaging is by far the most useful and easy wayto do it. The purpose of this study is to create a systemic database of all 13 districts of Dhaka Division for greenness analysis. In this tool we demonstrate how the greenness of 13 districts in Dhaka Division is changed in last 15 years using Moderate-resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer(MODIS) Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) data. NDVI values from 2001-2015 of MODIS are used to extract the greenness of this regions and to show greennesshas changed
